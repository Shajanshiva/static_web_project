Avenida Paulista

A vast green oasis in the heart of São Paulo, perfect for walking, cycling, and picnics.

The vibrant heart of Japanese culture in São Paulo, filled with lantern-lit streets and unique shops.
Explore authentic markets, temples, and festivals that celebrate Asian traditions.
