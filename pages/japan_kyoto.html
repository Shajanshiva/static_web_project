Top Activities & Places

Fushimi Inari Taisha

Kyoto’s most iconic shrine, famous for its thousands of vibrant red torii gates winding up the mountain.
A spiritual journey offering scenic trails, peaceful forests, and breathtaking views of the city.

Kinkaku-ji

A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf, reflecting beautifully on the surrounding pond.
One of Kyoto’s most iconic landmarks, set amidst serene gardens and lush greenery.

Gion District

Kyoto’s historic geisha district, known for its traditional wooden teahouses and cultural charm.
Stroll through lantern-lit streets and experience the elegance of old Japan.
