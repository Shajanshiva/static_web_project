Top Activities & Places

Eiffel Tower

Paris’s most iconic landmark, offering breathtaking views from its iron lattice structure.
A symbol of romance and innovation, illuminated beautifully against the night sky.

Tuileries Garden

A historic garden between the Louvre and Place de la Concorde, perfect for a leisurely stroll.
Admire its fountains, sculptures, and flowerbeds in the heart of Paris.

Sacré-Cœur Basilica

A stunning white-domed basilica perched atop Montmartre, offering sweeping views of Paris.
A place of peace and artistry, surrounded by charming streets and vibrant cafés.
