Top Activities & Places

La Sagrada Família

Antoni Gaudí’s unfinished masterpiece, blending Gothic and Art Nouveau styles in breathtaking detail.
A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most iconic landmarks of Barcelona.

Park Güell

A whimsical park designed by Antoni Gaudí, filled with vibrant mosaics, curved benches, and playful architecture.
Stroll through gardens and terraces offering panoramic views of Barcelona’s skyline.

La Rambla

Barcelona’s most famous boulevard, bustling with street performers, flower stalls, and open-air cafés.
A vibrant promenade that connects Plaça de Catalunya with the waterfront at Port Vell.
