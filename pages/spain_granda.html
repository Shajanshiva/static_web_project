Top Activities & Places

The Alhambra & Generalife

A magnificent Moorish palace and fortress complex, showcasing stunning Islamic art and architecture.
Explore lush gardens, intricate courtyards, and breathtaking views of Granada.

albaicín

The old Moorish quarter of Granada, with narrow winding streets and whitewashed houses.
Famous for its scenic viewpoints of the Alhambra and its vibrant Andalusian charm.

Flamenco in Sacromonte Caves

Experience authentic flamenco performances in the traditional cave houses of Sacromonte.
A passionate blend of music, dance, and culture that captures the soul of Andalusia.
