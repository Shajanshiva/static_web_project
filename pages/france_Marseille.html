Top Activities & Places

Vieux-Port (Old Port)

The historic heart of Marseille, lined with fishing boats, cafés, and bustling markets.
A vibrant waterfront hub where tradition meets Mediterranean charm.

Basilique Notre-Dame de la Garde

Perched on a hilltop, this iconic basilica overlooks Marseille with breathtaking panoramic views.
A symbol of faith and protection, admired for its Romanesque-Byzantine architecture.

Calanques National Park

A breathtaking stretch of limestone cliffs, turquoise waters, and hidden coves along the Mediterranean coast.
Perfect for hiking, boating, and swimming in one of France’s most stunning natural landscapes.
