Top Activities & Places

Shibuya Crossing

The world’s busiest pedestrian intersection, where hundreds cross in every direction at once.
Surrounded by neon lights, giant screens, and endless shopping and dining options.

Sensō-ji Temple

Tokyo’s oldest Buddhist temple, famous for its striking red gate and giant lantern.
A spiritual landmark surrounded by Nakamise Street’s traditional shops and street food.

Tokyo Tower

An iconic red-and-white landmark inspired by the Eiffel Tower, offering panoramic city views.
At night, its glowing lights create one of Tokyo’s most stunning skylines.
